Grilled Cheese!

One delicious looking Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • One tablesppon butter
  • Two slices of sharp Cheddar cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

  1. Heat skillet on med/high
  2. Butter bread/ both sides
  3. Place bread on hot skillet
  4. Turn over
  5. Add cheese
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. Make into sandwich
  8. Enjoy!
