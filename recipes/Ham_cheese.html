Ham and Cheese sandwich!

One delicious looking Ham and Cheese Sandwich!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of bread
  • One tablesppon butter
  • Two slices of sharp Cheddar cheese
  • Ham

    • Steps

  1. Heat skillet on med/high
  2. Butter bread/ both sides
  3. Place bread on hot skillet
  4. Turn over
  5. Add cheese
  6. Add ham
  7. Make into sandwich
  8. Enjoy!
